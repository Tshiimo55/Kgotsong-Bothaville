Spotlight #1 Bakery

Ba Rona Borotho Bakery

Ba Rona Borotho Bakery is more than just a place to buy bread — it's a symbol of community resilience and entrepreneurship in the heart of Kgotsong. Founded by the passionate Pastor Pitsi, this local bakery provides fresh, affordable baked goods while creating opportunity for the people around it.

Every loaf tells a story of hard work, faith, and commitment to uplifting the community. Ba Rona (meaning "ours" in Sesotho) truly belongs to all of Kgotsong.
